    How to Play Quantum Drifters

    • Move: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to navigate the quantum field
    • Objective: Collect quantum energy (blue orbs) to increase your score
    • Particles: You can phase through other players (particles)
    • Anti-Particles: Avoid red anti-particles - they will eliminate you!
    • Quantum State: Press SPACE to temporarily become intangible to anti-particles (cooldown applies)
    • Winning: The player with the highest score when the timer ends wins!
